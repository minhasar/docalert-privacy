Privacy Policy

App Name: DocAlert | Last Updated: May 7, 2026

1. Introduction

DocAlert is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the DocAlert mobile application.

2. Camera Permission

DocAlert requires camera access solely to capture document images for scanning. Images are processed on-device and are not transmitted to external servers. You can revoke camera access through your device settings at any time.

3. Information We Collect

  • Camera Data: Used only for document scanning. Not stored externally.
  • Scanned Documents: Stored locally on your device only. We do not upload or retain copies.
  • Technical Data: Basic crash and performance data (anonymised) to improve app stability.

4. How We Use Your Information

Information is used solely to provide document scanning functionality, diagnose technical issues, and improve app performance. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

5. Data Storage and Security

All scanned documents are stored locally on your device. We implement industry-standard security measures. We have no access to documents stored on your device.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase (Google) for crash analytics with anonymised data only. See Google Privacy Policy for details.

7. Children Privacy

DocAlert is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
